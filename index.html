Tour Details

Exciting and memorable tours!

Vacations in USA

1500$ - 5 Days

"Embark on an amazing adventure through America's most iconic landmarks, a journey full of diversity and excitement that takes you on a tour through history, culture, and breathtaking nature in 5 unforgettable days!"

This trip offers you a unique experience that combines adventure, history and stunning nature in just 5 days. Starting in New York City, you will enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of the city and explore its famous landmarks such as Times Square and Central Park. Then, head to Washington, DC, where you can enjoy the majestic historical landmarks such as the Lincoln Memorial and the National Air Museum. Next, you will head to the Grand Canyon in Arizona, where you will stand in awe of the beauty of the magnificent natural landscapes and unique mountainous terrain. On the last day, you will conclude your adventure in Las Vegas, where you will enjoy the bright nightlife and amazing entertainment shows. An amazing journey that takes you through the unique diversity of America, making it an unforgettable experience.

Day 1: Arrival at New York City

-Arrive in New York City, the bustling heart of the East Coast. Start your day with a visit to Times Square, then head to Central Park for a relaxing walk
-Visit the Empire State Building or Top of the Rock for spectacular views of the city skyline. End your night with a Broadway show or a dinner cruise to see the Statue of Liberty lit up.

Day 2: New York City to Washington, D.C.

-Take a ferry to visit the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island to learn about America's immigration history.
-Catch a train or flight to Washington, D.C. Upon arrival, start exploring the National Mall, including the Lincoln Memorial, Washington Monument, and U.S. Capitol.

Day 3: Washington, D.C. Exploration

-Visit the National Museum of American History and the National Air and Space Museum to learn more about the country’s evolution.
-Take a guided tour of the White House or Capitol Hill, or visit the U.S. Supreme Court.

Day 4: Travel to Grand Canyon

- Begin your exploration with a visit to the South Rim of the Grand Canyon. Hike along the Rim Trail for dramatic views or take a helicopter tour for a unique perspective of this natural wonder.
-Watch the sunset over the canyon—an unforgettable experience with colorful skies.

Day 5: Grand Canyon and Return to Las Vegas

- Head to Las Vegas. Spend the afternoon exploring the Las Vegas Strip, with its dazzling lights, iconic resorts, and attractions.
-CEnd your trip with a show, a visit to the Bellagio Fountains, or enjoy dinner at one of the city’s world-class restaurants.
